To sum up how many got the gift they wished for, the correct answer would be as following the query:

SELECT COUNT(DISTINCT user_wishlists.user_id) FROM gift_exchanges JOIN user_wishlists ON gift_exchanges.receiver_id = user_wishlists.user_id AND gift_exchanges.gift_name = user_wishlists.gift_name

The result is: 43
